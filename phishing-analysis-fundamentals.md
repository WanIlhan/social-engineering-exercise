# Exercise 2: Phishing Analysis Fundamentals

## Introduction
Phishing emails are crafted to appear legitimate in order to deceive users into sharing confidential information.

## Components of an Email
- Sender address
- Subject line
- Email body
- Embedded links and attachments

## Social Engineering Techniques Used
- Urgency
- Authority
- Trust manipulation

## Risks to Organizations
Phishing attacks can cause data breaches, financial damage, and reputational harm.

## Defensive Measures
- Email filtering systems
- Employee awareness training
- Strong password policies

## Conclusion
Analyzing phishing emails helps improve security awareness and reduces the risk of successful attacks.
